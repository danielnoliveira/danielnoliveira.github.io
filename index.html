Hi, my name is Daniel Nogueira

I’m a Mobile Software Developer with over 5 years of experience, working mainly with Flutter, React Native (RN) and Expo, building high-quality, scalable and high-performance mobile applications. I have experience developing both standard mobile applications and whitelabel solutions, across multiple business domains such as on-demand services, healthcare, chemical industry, self-service applications, casual games, chatbots, dating apps, and internal corporate platforms focused on employee communication and engagement. My technical background includes Firebase, offline-first architectures, real-time communication with sockets, REST APIs, payment gateway integrations, and OTA update strategies using Expo Updates and Shorebird. I also have solid experience with CI/CD pipelines using Codemagic, Fastlane, and Azure DevOps. I strongly value Clean Architecture, SOLID principles, Object-Oriented Programming, unit testing, and clean, maintainable code, always aiming to deliver sustainable solutions aligned with product and business goals. In my free time, I work on personal projects and study game development with Godot, API development with Go, and native mobile apps using Swift and Kotlin.
